  • Under 400 words:

    Hello, I hope you are doing well.
    Before you read further, I would like to say two things -

    1. I always find it hard to write about me - maybe because I can’t talk about 25 years of living in under 400 words.
    2. I like people who like to read (since you chose to read the long-bio version, I am assuming). So you’re already awesome in my view.

    I am a product maker, writer, cycle-enthusiast and a bibliophile. I graduated as an engineer and am currently working with Homzhub.

    I was starting my career at a company with a clean slate and since I hadn’t quite figured out what I wanted to do with my career yet, I intended to use this opportunity well.

    So, initially I took part in - business operations, strategising, customer support, product management, marketing, hiring too.

    Eventually, I realised I liked being a generalist.

    And I also loved building products. Maybe, that is why at Homzhub, I finally settled on as a role of Product Manager.

    Perhaps an important event in my life that I should really talk about is my journey with Make A Difference. I worked with some of the best youth across 24 cities in the country. I learned so much from being part of such an amazing team working towards a common cause that I really won’t be able to put into words.
    I think it is safe to say, I got more out of volunteering my time at this non-profit than I could ever hope to repay.

    I have tried to other things outside my 9 to 5 too (working at a startup is anything but that). I made an app for real estate agents and I tried to launch an under-wear company which never saw the light of the day. I freelanced a bit, just to improve my coding skills. Working in customer support teaches you one thing, an angry customer is a powerful motivating factor.

    I also read quite a bit. I am amongst those who believe, reading great books is a cheat code to living. I read a lot of biographies and memoirs, when I was younger. These days I read on history a bit. Favourite book? - Atlas Shrugged by Ayn Rand. I don’t know why, it just is.

    That is it I think. About me in under 400 words. If you’ve read till here, I would like to thank you for taking interest in my story. Maybe you can mail me, I would surely like to connect with you.
